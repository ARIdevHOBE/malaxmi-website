Who We Are

Transforming Spaces, Creating Experiences

MA LAXMI SALE & SERVICE is a premier interior design and decoration company based in Durgapur, West Bengal. We specialize in creating stunning residential and commercial interiors that combine functionality with aesthetic beauty.

With years of industry experience and a passionate team of designers and craftsmen, we have established ourselves as a trusted partner for homeowners and businesses seeking to elevate their spaces.

From false ceiling installations and wall treatments to modular furniture and complete interior makeovers, we deliver end-to-end solutions with meticulous attention to detail and an unwavering commitment to quality.
